When to Call Emergency:

  • Severe tooth pain that prevents sleep
  • Facial swelling or signs of infection
  • Knocked-out or broken teeth
  • Uncontrolled bleeding from mouth
  • Jaw injury or suspected fracture

After-Hours Protocol

For non-emergency issues after hours, leave a detailed voicemail or send a WhatsApp message. We'll respond first thing the next business day.
