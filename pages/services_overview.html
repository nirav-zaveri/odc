Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our dental services

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend regular checkups every 6 months for optimal oral health. However, some patients may need more frequent visits based on their individual needs and risk factors.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Our team will help verify your coverage and maximize your benefits. We also offer flexible payment plans for treatments not covered by insurance.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Professional teeth whitening is completely safe when performed by qualified dentists. We use advanced techniques that protect your enamel while achieving excellent results.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call our emergency hotline immediately at +91 98765 43210. For knocked-out teeth, try to reinsert gently or keep in milk. For severe pain, rinse with warm salt water and take over-the-counter pain relief.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care and regular maintenance, dental implants can last 20-30 years or even a lifetime. They have a success rate of over 95% and are considered the gold standard for tooth replacement.
