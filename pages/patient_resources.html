Dental Emergency Guide

Quick reference for common dental emergencies with step-by-step first aid instructions

Severe Toothache

1 Rinse mouth with warm salt water
2 Apply cold compress to outside of cheek
3 Take over-the-counter pain medication
4 Call immediately for emergency appointment
Call Emergency

Knocked Out Tooth

1 Handle tooth by crown, not root
2 Rinse gently with water if dirty
3 Try to reinsert or store in milk
4 See dentist within 30 minutes
Call Emergency

Broken/Chipped Tooth

1 Save any broken pieces
2 Rinse mouth with warm water
3 Apply gauze to control bleeding
4 Use cold compress for swelling
Call Emergency
